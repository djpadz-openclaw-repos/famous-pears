# Famous Peers - Duo Validation Criteria

## Core Rules

A valid duo must meet ALL of these criteria:

1. **Famous** - The duo must be well-known/recognizable
2. **Exactly 2 elements** - Must be a pair, not more, not less
3. **Has a name** - There's a name/label that refers to the duo
4. **Answer not in name** - The duo name should NOT contain the answers themselves
   - ❌ "Romeo and Juliet" (answers are in the name)
   - ✅ "Warring families in Romeo and Juliet" (Montague and Capulet)

## Invalid Categories

### Abstract Concepts
- Good and Evil, Day and Night, Plus and Minus, Male and Female, Sun and Moon, Left and Right, Big and Small, Hot and Cold, Happy and Sad, Life and Death
- ❌ Not famous duos, just abstract concepts

### Food/Object Pairings
- Peanut Butter and Jelly, Salt and Pepper, Bacon and Eggs, Bread and Butter, Shoes and Socks
- ❌ Not famous duos

### Mixed Entity Types
- Chester Carlson and Haloid (person + company)
- ❌ Cannot mix people with companies or non-person entities

### Single Entities
- Superman, The Terminator, Rambo, Rocky, Jaws, Alien, Predator
- ❌ Single characters/movies, not duos

### Artist Collaborations (Not Permanent Duos)
- David Bowie and Mick Jagger (one collaboration)
- James Brown and George Clinton (collaborators)
- Miles Davis and John Coltrane (collaborators)
- Marvin Gaye and Tammi Terrell (separate artists who collaborated)
- Ike and Tina Turner (separate careers)
- Johnny Cash and June Carter (separate careers)
- ❌ Not a famous duo, just collaborators or separate artists

### Non-Duos (Just Two Famous People)
- Leonardo da Vinci and Michelangelo (two artists, not a duo)
- Alexander the Great and Aristotle (teacher-student, not a duo)
- ❌ Not a famous duo, just two famous people

### Movies with 3+ Principal Characters
- Ghostbusters (4 characters)
- Goodfellas (ensemble cast)
- The Matrix (3+ characters)
- Indiana Jones (unclear co-star)
- Titanic (ensemble)
- Jaws (ensemble)
- ❌ Too many principal characters

## Valid Categories

### Musical Duos
- Simon and Garfunkel (Paul Simon, Art Garfunkel)
- Steely Dan (Donald Fagen, Walter Becker)
- The Black Keys (Dan Auerbach, Patrick Carney)
- ✅ Famous musical duos with exactly 2 members

### Comedy Duos
- Laurel and Hardy (Stan Laurel, Oliver Hardy)
- Abbott and Costello (Bud Abbott, Lou Costello)
- ✅ Famous comedy duos

### Fictional Character Pairs
- Tom and Jerry (cartoon characters)
- Batman and Robin (fictional characters)
- Mickey and Minnie Mouse (cartoon characters)
- ✅ Famous fictional duos

### Historical/Legendary Pairs
- Lewis and Clark (explorers)
- Bonnie and Clyde (outlaws)
- ✅ Famous historical duos

### Business Founders
- Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak (Apple founders)
- Hewlett and Packard (HP founders)
- Bill Gates and Paul Allen (Microsoft founders)
- ✅ Famous business duos

### Actors Playing Same Role (Across Versions)
- "Actors who played Rooster Cogburn" (John Wayne, Jeff Bridges)
- "Actors who played Vito Corleone" (Marlon Brando, Robert DeNiro)
- ✅ Valid if exactly 2 actors played the same iconic role

### Movies with Exactly 2 Principal Characters
- Die Hard (John McClane, Hans Gruber)
- Toy Story (Woody, Buzz Lightyear)
- Silence of the Lambs (Clarice Starling, Hannibal Lecter)
- ✅ Valid if duo name clarifies what's being asked (character names or actor names)

### Technical/Scientific Pairs
- Battery terminals (anode, cathode)
- ✅ Famous technical pairs with exactly 2 elements

## Member Definition Rules

- Members must be clearly defined (either character names, actor names, or entity names)
- Do NOT mix member types (e.g., don't have one actor name and one character name)
- For movies, clarify in the duo name whether asking for character names or actor names
  - Example: "Main characters in Die Hard" (John McClane, Hans Gruber)
  - Example: "Actors who played Rooster Cogburn" (John Wayne, Jeff Bridges)
